About Eye Checkup

An eye checkup is a routine examination of your vision and overall eye health. During the checkup, an eye specialist tests how well you can see, checks for vision problems like nearsightedness or farsightedness, and looks for early signs of eye conditions such as glaucoma or cataracts. Regular eye exams help detect issues early and ensure your eyes stay healthy.

What We Check

Vision Clarity

Assessing how clearly you see near and far.

Eye Pressure

Checking intraocular pressure to prevent glaucoma.

Retina Health

Examining retina and optic nerve for early disease detection.

Lens Condition

Inspecting eye lens for cataracts and clarity issues.
